Find an LU factorization for matrix A

Find matrices L and U such as A=LU
In case row reordering is necessary, A is factorized as PA=LU
This is called LU factorization with Partial Pivoting.
In case both row and column reordering are necessary, A is factorized as PAQ=LU
This is called LU factorization with full pivoting .
